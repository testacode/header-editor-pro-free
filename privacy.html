Privacy Policy - Header Editor Pro - Free

Your Privacy Matters: This extension is designed with privacy first. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Collection

Header Editor Pro - Free does not collect, store, or transmit any personal user data to external servers. The extension operates entirely within your browser and does not communicate with any remote services.

Local Storage

All header configurations, profiles, and settings are stored locally on your device using the browser's storage API. This data includes:

  • Header modification rules and profiles
  • Extension settings and preferences
  • Profile names and configurations

This data never leaves your computer and is only accessible by the extension itself. You can clear this data at any time by removing the extension or clearing your browser data.

Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions to function:

  • declarativeNetRequest: To intercept and modify HTTP headers
  • storage: To save your configurations locally
  • activeTab: To apply header modifications to the current tab
  • tabs: To manage header rules across browser tabs

These permissions are used solely for the extension's functionality and not for data collection.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the extension and the updated date below.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or the extension's data practices, please contact us through the extension's support channels on GitHub.
